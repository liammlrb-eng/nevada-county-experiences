How to use this site

There's no wrong way to explore — pick the path that fits.

You already know what you want
Use the search box on the Explore tab — type it and press Find. Synonyms are built in, so "metalsmith" still finds a studio listed as "blacksmithing".
You're browsing — inspire me
On Explore, pick one or more vibes (Historic, Arts, Foodie, Active…). Cards filter instantly; combine vibes to widen, add the date filter to narrow.
Plan it for me
Open the Help Me Plan tab. Answer a few questions — days, pace, who's coming, activity level, interests — and get a tailored day-by-day draft you can edit.
You want a ready-made trip
Open Suggested — hand-picked itineraries from us. Hit Customize this trip on any of them to copy it into your own planner and tweak it.
Tell me what's worth knowing
Local Guides are long-form pieces written by people who live here — historians, hikers, musicians, tribal members — on the places and stories that don't show up in a venue card.
You have set dates
Set the date range in the filter bar (or hit Today / Weekend / Week), then flip the Explore tab to Calendar view to see exactly what's on each day.
Good to know
• Anything you add lands in My Itinerary — organize it by day, view it on a map (with parking & restrooms), or share a link.
• The strip near the top flags what's In Season this week — wildflowers, swimming season, festivals.
• Filters stack: vibes + browse-mode + date range all narrow together.
