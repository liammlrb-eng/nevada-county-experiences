❓ How to find what you want

There's no wrong way to explore — pick the path that fits you.

🔍
You already know what you want
Use the Search box at the top — type it and press Find. Synonyms are built in, so "metalsmith" still finds a studio listed as "blacksmithing".
🎭
You're browsing — inspire me
Pick one or more vibes in the Browse by Vibe section (Historic, Arts, Foodie, Active…). Cards filter instantly; combine vibes to widen, add Area/Category/date to narrow.
✨
Plan it for me
Open the ✨ Help Me Plan tab. Answer a few questions — days, pace, who's coming, activity level, interests — and get a tailored day-by-day draft you can edit.
📅
You have set dates
Set the When? filter, then open Upcoming Events → Calendar to see exactly what's on each day of your visit.
🧭
You want a ready-made trip
Open Suggested Experiences — curated itineraries. Hit Customize this trip on any of them to copy it into your own planner and tweak it.
🏠
You just need a place to stay
Tap the 🏠 Lodging tile alongside the vibes — it jumps straight to every hotel, B&B, cabin and campground.
Good to know
• Anything you add lands in My Itinerary — organize it by day, view it on a map (with parking & restrooms), or share a link.
• The In Season Now strip near the top flags what's special this week — wildflowers, swimming season, festivals.
• Filters stack: Area + Category + dates + a vibe all narrow together.
